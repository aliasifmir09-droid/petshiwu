# HARO / Connectively Setup for PetShiwu
*2 minutes. High-authority backlinks from journalist placements.*

---

## What is HARO?

Help A Reporter Out (HARO), now called **Connectively**, connects journalists with expert sources. When a journalist is writing an article about pets and needs an expert quote, they post a query. You respond with a brief, helpful answer. If they use your quote, they link back to petshiwu.com.

**Why it matters:**
- Links from major publications (Forbes, Inc, NYT, Huffington Post) are extremely high-authority
- A single HARO placement can drive meaningful traffic + rankings
- Free, takes 5–10 min per day

---

## Signup Steps (Pet does this — 2 minutes)

1. **Go to:** https://www.connectively.us
2. **Click "Sign Up" → choose "Source"** (you're providing expert input, not pitching stories)
3. **Email:** support@petshiwu.com (or Pet's main email — pick whichever you'll check daily)
4. **Password:** secure
5. **Profile setup:**
   - Name: "Pet Shiwu" or Pet's name
   - Title: "Founder, PetShiwu" or "NYC Pet Supply Expert"
   - Bio: "Founder of PetShiwu, NYC's local pet supply delivery service. Expert on urban pet ownership, pet nutrition, NYC pet safety, and small-breed care. Based in Jackson Heights, Queens."
   - Categories: Pets & Animals, Consumer Products, Health, Lifestyle
6. **Subscribe to:** Daily digest (emails arrive ~3 times per day)
7. **Verify email**

Done. Queries start arriving immediately.

---

## Daily Routine (5–10 min)

**Email arrives 3x daily:** 5:30 AM, 12:30 PM, 5:30 PM ET

For each query:
1. **Read carefully** — does it relate to pets, NYC, delivery, pet nutrition, dog food, cat food?
2. **If yes:** Reply within 2 hours with a 100–200 word helpful answer
3. **Include:** petshiwu.com URL + your bio at the bottom
4. **Be helpful, not promotional** — answer the question, mention petshiwu as one data point
